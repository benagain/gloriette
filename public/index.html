Our Gin

Introducing our Gloriette Gin

We have a small distillery where we create our exquisite small batch vacuum distilled Gloriette Gins. Developed and perfected in the shadow of the mighty ruins of Corfe Castle on the beautiful Isle of Purbeck.

The name Gloriette is inspired by the Gloriette Palace that once stood within Corfe Castle. It was a secluded place reserved for the pleasure and enjoyment of the King’s inner circle and most distinguished guests.

Full of elegance and poise, and with every detail crafted in harmony; our Gloriette Gins bring the spirit of the Gloriette Palace to life with enticing tastes, aromas and flavours that are both delicious and intriguing.

We invite you to step away from your day to day and become immersed in special moments with those who enrich your life.

We would like to present our Gloriette Artisan, King’s Cut and Sipping Gins.

Gloriette Artisan Gin

Our delicious award-winning Gloriette Artisan Gin is crafted using a fine and intricate blend of 45 botanicals that bring a level of depth and complexity that is simply beautiful.

This exquisite gin captivates the palate with a refreshing explosion of flavour that is balanced with a subtle heat, smooth aromatic complexity, and a lingering finish that is curiously moreish!

Our Artisan Gin recipe won London Spirits Competition Silver Medal place in 2022 and received this accolade from the judges:

“Floral and delicate fruity notes on the nose and palate. Well-rounded with a pleasantly long aftertaste”.

 

 

Gloriette King’s Gin

Our Navy strength Gin is exceptionally robust. Enjoy King’s Cut Gin with Mediterranean tonic and a slice of lime to allow the enchanting flavours to enliven the palate.

 

 

 

 

 

 

Gloriette Sipping Gin

Our Sipping Gin is a unique experience with its earthy sweetness and aromatic depth, pair with Sicilian lemon or ginger ale and a slice of lemon. Or perhaps for those who prefer to savour their drink: serve simply on the rocks to savour the exotic mixture of fruits and botanicals.
